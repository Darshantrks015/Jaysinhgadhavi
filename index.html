Biography

Jaysinh Gadhavi, also known as FIRESINH, began his musical journey in 2013, learning music at Saptak Music School. He furthered his knowledge in 2014 at MS University in Baroda.

He started performing live shows in 2015. In 2015, he appeared on the TV reality show The Voice India.
FIRESINH completed a vibrant Pre-Navratri tour from 2023-2024, which included:
• 2 shows in Canada (2024)
• 2 shows in the UK (2024)
• 4 shows in Australia (2023)
• 5 shows in Canada (2023)
He performs over 100 shows annually, both nationally and internationally.Since 2019, he has been performing as a lead singer at large-scale Navratri events in Surat, Gujarat.
